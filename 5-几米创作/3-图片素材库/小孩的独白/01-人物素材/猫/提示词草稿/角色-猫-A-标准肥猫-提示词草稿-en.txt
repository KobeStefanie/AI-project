An adult blue-gray chubby cat. Body rounded but not bloated — not morbidly obese, just the natural roundness of eating well and sleeping well. From the side the belly sags just slightly, would sway gently when walking. Round head, cheek fur puffing out a bit, making the face look slightly larger than it actually is. Ears neither large nor small, tips slightly rounded, a small pale pink patch inside the right ear.

Eyes: almond-shaped, half-lidded — not sleepy, just the "I know you're looking at me but I can't be bothered" expression. Pupils two thin slits. Irises gray-green, very low saturation, barely standing out against the blue-gray fur. Eyes set slightly wider apart than a typical cat, giving a slightly slow-reacting look — not stupid, just half a beat behind.

Fur: short-haired but dense. Wet-on-wet watercolor base — blue-gray dominant, darkest along the spine, gradually lightening to gray-white toward the belly. Colored pencil cross-hatching on the back and tail suggesting fur texture. Tail thick, tip slightly curled, fur at the tip paler than the body.

Legs: short and stout, four white socks — front paws white to the wrist, hind paws white to the ankle. From the side, a low center of gravity when walking.

Overall: a cat that doesn't seem to care much about anything. Not cold — its world is just small, slow, and quiet.
