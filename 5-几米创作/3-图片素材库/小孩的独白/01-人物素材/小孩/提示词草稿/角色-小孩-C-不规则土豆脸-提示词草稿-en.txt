Face shape (irregular organic form): this face cannot be described with any geometric shape — not round, not square, not oval. The forehead's left side is slightly higher than the right. This doesn't look like a "drawn" face, but a "grown" face — the shape a plant takes when it grows by itself in a corner, asymmetrical, unrefined, fitting no standard.

The ultimate Jimmy Liao "happens to look exactly like this" — uningratiating, non-standard, imperfect, but you can't stop looking.
